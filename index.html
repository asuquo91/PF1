About Me

Hello my name is Ben Etim, a software developer with a finance and accounting background. My journey from the world of finance to coding has fostered a unique blend of analytical thinking and a strong attention to detail. Proficient in JavaScript, Python, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and React, I thrive on building elegant and responsive applications.

What sets me apart is my insatiable thirst for learning. Technology's dynamic nature inspires me to continuously expand my skill set and tackle new challenges. As a software developer, I'm not just writing code; I'm crafting solutions that make an impact.

I'm enthusiastic about collaborating on innovative projects, embracing new challenges, and contributing to the ever-evolving tech landscape. Let's connect and explore the endless possibilities ahead in the world of software engineering.

Python Recipe App

The Recipe is a Python-based application featuring a user-friendly interface built with tkinter. The app empowers users to effortlessly create, edit, and organize their cherished recipes. Key functionalities include recipe creation, editing, and deletion, along with the ability to filter recipes by dietary preferences such as vegetarian, vegan, and all-inclusive. Throughout this project, I honed my skills in Python developmen &, GUI design using tkinter. It provided a solid foundation for future endeavors in creating practical software solutions.
